We need volunteers!

We have a need for tutors, classroom assistants and behind-the-scenes, administrative volunteers.

Tutor Training

Training Effective Literacy Tutors (TELT) Tutor Training Workshops are offered over two consecutive Saturdays by the Portland Literacy Council. BLC volunteers are eligible for a reduced registration fee of $20.00.

Preregistration is required. Contact Portland Literacy Council at 503.244.6111 (x3700) or pdxliteracy@hotmail.com.
