Providing low cost ESL, literacy, and citizenship classes through a network of trained volunteer tutors.

Take an Adult ESL Class

Next placements tests will be in August and September 2015.

All students who are new to the English for Speakers of Other Languages (ESOL) program are required to take a placement test.

Cada estudiante nuevo en el programa de clases de inglés (ESL) ha de examinarse. Queremos asegurar el nivel correcto para usted -- ni demasiado fácil ni demasiado difícil. Tenemos seis niveles de inglés y una clase de ciudadismo.

Our Adult ESL Program

  • 6 levels of English
  • 2 class meetings per week
  • Morning or evening in locations around Beaverton
  • Reduced fees available to those with financial need.

Help Out. Volunteer

Volunteer, make a difference! The community's need exceeds our current capability. We have opportunities to work directly with students as a tutor as well as behind the scnes on the administrative side of the organization.

Tutor Training

Training Effective Literacy Tutors (TELT) Tutor Training Workshops are offered over two consecutive Saturdays by the Portland Literacy Council. BLC volunteers are eligible for a reduced registration fee of $20.00.

Preregistration is required. Contact Portland Literacy Council at 503.244.6111 (x3700) or pdxliteracy@hotmail.com.
